  • The bearded dragon is a species of lizard known for its flared throat.


  • In the wild, beardies enjoy a lifespan of 10 to 15 years; in captivity, they can live up to 20 years.


  • They can be red, orange, brown, tan, silvery blue, white, solid, or patterned.


  • Bearded dragons have stiff scales, smooth skin, and spikes.


  • Beardies are omnivores, eating more live prey when younger and gradually becoming vegetarian as they age.
