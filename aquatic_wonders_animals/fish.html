Mandarinfish, also known as mandarin dragonet, is a small, brightly colored member of the dragonet family. They are native to the Pacific from the Ryukyu Islands to Australia. The mandarin fish gets its name from the brightly-colored robes of the Imperial Chinese mandarin.

They can be shades of blue, orange, green, and yellow. Their bright colors actaully deter predators away. Also, instead of scales they are covered in mucus that protects their body.
